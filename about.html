guest@debug-diary:~$ cat mission.txt

Debug Diary exists because coders rarely talk about the mistakes behind the scenes. We share the working projects, the clever solutions, the wins—but keep quiet about the late-night debugging sessions, the stubborn bugs, and the moments that made us question if we really knew what we were doing.

This is a collection of those moments. The real ones. The confusing ones. The “how did I even write that?” ones. The kind that, in hindsight, seem obvious—but at the time, felt like the computer had its own agenda.

guest@debug-diary:~$ cat philosophy.txt

Every bug was tackled. Every fix came with a lesson. The frustration eased (eventually). The knowledge mostly stuck.

If you’ve stumbled over similar mistakes: you’re not alone. If you haven’t yet: don’t worry—you probably will.
